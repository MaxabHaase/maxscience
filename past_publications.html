I have maintained interests in yeast taxonomy, bacterial lipid transfer, deep mutational scanning, syntheic biology, and genome evolution. The below publications reflect these interests and the many fantastic colaborative projects I have been a part of.

Humanized yeasts

During my Phd I developed a histone humanized yeast model. We used this system to see how yeast evolve with human histones, what variants histone function in yeast, and what yeast kinetochore components are replaceable with human homologs.

  • Haase MAB, Lazar-Stefanita L, Ólafsson G, Wudzinska A, Shen MJ, Truong DM, Boeke JD@. (2024). macroH2A1 drives nucleosome dephasing and genome instability in histone humanized yeast. Cell Reports
