About Me

I am an American evolutionary biologist with interests in how cells divide, how chromosomes organize, and how genomes evolve. I received my PhD from New York University where I was advised by Jef Boeke.

I am now a MSCA Postdoctoral Fellow with Andrea Musacchio at the MPI in Dortmund investigating the atomic structure of native kinetochore molecules.
